Dungeon Survivor

High Concept

This a survival game where you play as knight trapped in a dungeon.

Genre

Survival Game

Platform

Desktop only

Story

The main character is a knight trying to survive as long as possible in a dungeon while figuring out a way to escape. He is constantly being attacked by monsters while being trapped.

Esthetics

Graphic Style: Pixelated

Sound: There will be sound for when the knight attacks, takes damage and receives an item. There will also be sound for when enemies get damaged and when items spawn in.

Gameplay

Mechanics

The main objective is to survive as long as possible. The player will need to learn how to avoid enemies and attack at the best time, which would usually be when they are powered up by one of the items.

Controls

The game will be played only using the Keyboard. Players can use WASD to move and Space to attack

Onboarding

The basic controls and objectives of the game will be displayed on the starting screen.
